## Emergent Behaviors

### 1. Need-Driven Goal Creation
When hunger drops below 50%:
- Automatically creates "Satisfy Hunger" goal
- Priority scales with urgency
- Activates if slots available
- Directs attention to food
- Generates hunger-related thoughts

### 2. Goal-Directed Attention
Active goals guide attention:
- Goal keywords boost salience
- Voluntary attention shifts
- Intentional focus emerges
- Goal-relevant items prioritized

### 3. Uncertainty Expression
Low confidence beliefs:
- "I think..." (medium confidence)
- "I'm not sure, but..." (low confidence)
- "I might be wrong, but..." (very low confidence)
- Natural doubt expression

### 4. Belief Revision
New evidence updates beliefs:
- Player waves → "Player is friendly" (0.8)
- Player attacks → Revise to 0.4
- Confidence decreases
- Thought: "I'm not sure about the player anymore"

### 5. Contradiction Resolution
Conflicting beliefs detected:
- "Player is friendly" vs "Player is hostile"
- Scores beliefs by reliability
- Weakens lower-scoring belief
- Maintains coherent belief state

### 6. Coherent Action
Goal conflicts resolved:
- "Find food" vs "Flee danger"
- Safety > Survival
- Flee first, then find food
- Coherent behavior maintained
